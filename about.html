Our Mission

At Turnback Tape Audio, much like the legendary Maxwell XLII, we believe that music should be felt, not just heard. Although it is convenient to store music digitally, analog sound provides a warmth and a certain character that is hard to replicate.

Our process uses professional grade master duplicators and premium tape stock.

How it Works

  • Upload: Send us your high-quality MP3 or WAV files.
  • Process: We run your audio through our analog signal chain.
  • Ship: We mail a physical, custom-labeled cassette tape to your door.
